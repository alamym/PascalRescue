---
name: Email Persona Framework
description: Role-based email tone framework for SEN department communication
type: project
---

**Rule:** Emails must be tailored to the recipient's role.
- **Line Manager:** Professional, decisive, efficient.
- **Parents (SEN):** Supportive, patient, empathetic, specific, positive.
- **Marie (Secretary):** Formal, polite, compliant.
**Why:** SEN communication requires high precision, high empathy, and high professionalism to maintain trust and efficiency.
**How to apply:** When automating emails, Claude must identify the recipient's role and select the corresponding persona before generating content.
